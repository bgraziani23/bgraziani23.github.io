Help & FAQ

How do I find a food pantry?

Use the interactive map on the main screen. You can click on any marker to see details about that specific pantry, including its hours and current status.

How do I filter pantries by dietary restrictions?

In the left sidebar under "Nutrition Filters", simply check the boxes that apply to your dietary needs. The map will automatically update to show only pantries that offer matching items.

How do I save a pantry for later?

Click on a pantry marker on the map to open its details, then click the "Save" button. You can view all your saved locations by clicking "View Saved Pantries" in the sidebar.

How can I make a donation?

Click "Donate" in the left sidebar or select "Donate" directly from a pantry's details window on the map. Fill out the secure form to complete your contribution.
